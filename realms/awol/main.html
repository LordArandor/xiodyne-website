AWOL

  As the remaining Factions of Europe fight amongst themselves with dwindiling resources, there are those that believe a solution is to be found within the vast underground complex hiding under the nose of every Faction politican. Built before the Fall, the Labyrinth could contain treaures beyond your wildest dreams, or monsters beyond your most terrifying nightmares.

  A World Of Labyrinths. Or should I name it Dunkelheit? I don't know anymore. This is one of my earliest realms, and for the longest time something I wanted to craft a game around. The game never did work out, but I've still done a lot of worldbuilding.
